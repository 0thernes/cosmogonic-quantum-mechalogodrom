## Non-goals (core sim v0.x)

- Multiplayer / networking in the **deterministic instrument** — the cosmos is a single-machine,
  single-user simulation by design today.
- User accounts, telemetry exfiltration, or PII inside the sim loop — privacy scope stays nil.
- A general-purpose engine — this is a focused, opinionated art-simulation, not a framework.

These non-goals apply to the **live sim kernel**. Product layers in
[docs/KANBAN-2026-06-26.md](./docs/KANBAN-2026-06-26.md) (waitlist, hosted multiplayer,
subscriptions) are an **aspirational horizon** that wraps the dome without changing determinism.
