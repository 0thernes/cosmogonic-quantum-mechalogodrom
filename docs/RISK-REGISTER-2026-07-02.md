## Release gate on risk

Do not tag a release with any **H residual** risk unmitigated and undocumented. R1 and R2 are the
standing high-residual items; they are research/validation risks, not defects, and are acceptable for a
research artifact **as long as they are stated honestly** on every public surface (which they are).
