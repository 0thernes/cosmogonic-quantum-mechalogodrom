## 1 · Product statement

Cosmogonic Quantum Mechalogodrom is a **deterministic, browser-native, multi-substrate Artificial-Life
and cognition testbed** — a "petri dish for NHSI digital biologics and proto-sentience research." It runs
a seeded cosmos of up to 50,000 entities, an apex "Super Creature" cognition stack instrumented against
published consciousness theories, and the Tsotchke MIT quantum/scientific-computing corpus as its
non-transformer substrate. It is a **research artifact and a demonstrable experience**, not a product for
sale and not a claim of sentience.

## 3 · Success metrics (what "good" means)

- **Honesty:** every load-bearing number is measured (`file:line` or gate receipt); zero overclaims
  survive an adversarial audit. Butlin framing stays "8/14 met + 6/14 partial, NOT sentient."
  A score may only rise 1:1 with an ablation-verified gate — and, as the 2026-07-16 re-audit
  established, it must FALL as soon as the source stops supporting it (see §6).
- **Determinism:** identical seed → bit-identical run, always (machine-verified).
- **Health:** `bun run check` green — 3,303 tests, 84.64% line / 82.21% func portable floor,
  0 tsc/lint suppressions. (Receipt tokens; propagated by `scripts/sync-surfaces.ts` from
  `scripts/canonical-receipts.ts` — never hand-edit them here.)
- **Experience:** 60 fps at the desktop tier; the app boots and self-recovers on GitHub Pages with no
  frontend secrets (see [BENCHMARKS-2026-06-26.md](./BENCHMARKS-2026-06-26.md)).
- **Standing:** the A-Life comparative matrix holds Cosmogonic's breadth as a measured outlier against a
  129-system / 128-peer survey — breadth `3.84 / 5`, rank `#1/129`, population z `+3.220`, at a
  self-assessed peer maturity of `1.5/5` against a survey median of `3.5`. Breadth is generality, not
  quality, and the z is a **denominator-sensitive** statistic: growing the corpus moves it without any
  capability changing. See [NHSI-PROGRESS-DASHBOARD-2026-06-26.md](./NHSI-PROGRESS-DASHBOARD-2026-06-26.md)
  and [reports/2026-07-01-25-POINT-SCRUTINY-SCORECARD.md](./reports/2026-07-01-25-POINT-SCRUTINY-SCORECARD.md).

## 4 · Scope

**In scope:** deterministic sim core; apex + pantheon cognition; Tsotchke substrate wiring; WebGL
rendering; the observability/receipts stack; the docs/reports corpus; GitHub Pages deploy.

**Out of scope (explicit non-goals):**

- **Not a claim of consciousness/sentience/AGI/ASI** — those are research _targets_ and an architecture
  _program_, never achieved capability. The hard problem is untouched.
- **Not an LLM/transformer** — the substrate is deterministic + Tsotchke quantum math, by mandate.
- **Not a quantum-advantage claim** — the quantum math is exact classical simulation; a physical QPU
  would add speed/scale, not correctness.
- **Not commercial / not multi-user / not a backend product** — single-machine, static-deployable,
  All-Rights-Reserved. No frontend secrets; managed cloud only if ever added.
- **No world-first claim** — novelty is by integration, not primacy.

## 5 · Constraints (binding)

Determinism (seeded `Rng`, no `Math.random`/`Date.now` in sim) · living-docs SSOT · full gate before
commit · no PRs (local→`main`) · proprietary license · public/private boundary (see
[SECURITY-ARCHITECTURE-2026-07-02.md](./SECURITY-ARCHITECTURE-2026-07-02.md)). These are enforced by the
three MASTER files and `bun run check`.

## 6 · Open product bets (tracked, unproven)

- **Coupling > count** — the emergence thesis, and still the headline unproven bet.
  `meanAbsCoupling ≈ 0.27` against a gate floor of `0.188` (`coupling-audit.test.ts`) and an honesty
  ceiling of `< 0.6`. It has climbed (0.167 → 0.183 → 0.197 → 0.270) but `0.27 ≪ 0.5`, the
  dense-binding threshold: the ~30 deep-wired faculties co-activate without forming a tightly bound
  mind. The repo's own phrasing stands — _a pile is not a mind_. See
  [COMPLEXITY-2026-06-26.md](./COMPLEXITY-2026-06-26.md) and
  [BRAIN-NEUROLOGY-CONSCIOUSNESS-ENGINEERING-ASSESSMENT-2026-07-06.md](./BRAIN-NEUROLOGY-CONSCIOUSNESS-ENGINEERING-ASSESSMENT-2026-07-06.md).
- **Reproduction without selection** — heredity is live and lineage-verified (`entities.ts` `breedTraits`),
  but no birth or death path reads a heritable trait: birth is a flat trait-blind roll and lifespan is
  never inherited, so the population drifts rather than evolves. This is why the reproduction axis was
  corrected 4.0 → 3.5 on 2026-07-16 rather than defended. Closing it means making one reproductive
  quantity depend on a gene and gating it (`GATE-REPRO-SELECT`) — a product bet, not a bug.
- **External validation** — no third party has reproduced a claim yet; the reproducibility artifact is
  the next product milestone (see [RISK-REGISTER-2026-07-02.md](./RISK-REGISTER-2026-07-02.md)).
