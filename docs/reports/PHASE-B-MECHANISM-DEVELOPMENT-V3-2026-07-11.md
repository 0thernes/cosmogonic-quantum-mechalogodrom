## Production boundary hardened

  malformed restores are atomic and the 32-mind runtime cap is enforced.
- NHI world actions return `effectApplied`, `factSupported`, affected-body count, and exact energy
  transfer separately. The orchestrator validates action-specific evidence; the mind then reports
  whether its internal GOAP facts actually transitioned, so repeated evidence is never mislabeled.
- One percept/think/apply/acknowledge exception cannot starve later minds; even a failing diagnostic
  callback is captured in the exceptional report while the population beat advances.
- NHI identity, policy, and direct action sampling use dedicated deterministic streams. Material
  organism creation intentionally still advances the ordinary EntityManager physics/genome streams;
  the code and launch receipts no longer claim otherwise.

  attempts to remove mind, maps, target, wallet, body, and spawned entity without fabricating a death

  absence postconditions, and `logicalRollbackComplete` instead of assuming success. Scene/GPU cleanup

  material children or suppress their aggregate acknowledgement. Titan procreation likewise reports

  `512`-organism frame budget. Null/throwing spawns refund their reservation; at tiers capped at 5,000

- Optional audio, HUD, audit, and same-frame grid indexing are guarded so their failure cannot erase
  an already-material action. Social-audio cooldown is committed before its external sink runs, and
  ordinary spawn audio cannot turn a completed entity insertion into a half-transaction.
