## Recommended layered architecture (micro ↔ macro)

- **Environment:** RD biomass + CA hazards + influence-map textures (threat/territory/scent).
- **Perception:** spatial hash + field sampling.
- **Micro-brain:** genome-encoded tiny MLP → utility/needs arbiter (primary) → fuzzy mood → FSM/BT leaf → steering/boids.
- **Macro:** blackboard bus; Louvain tribes; GOAP titans; Markov weather/economy regimes.
- **Evolution:** rtNEAT-style reproduction at birth events — stable-sort before selection (biggest desync risk).
