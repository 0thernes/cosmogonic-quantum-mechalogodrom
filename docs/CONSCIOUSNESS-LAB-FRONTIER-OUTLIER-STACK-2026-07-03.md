# Consciousness Lab Frontier Outlier Stack

**Date:** 2026-07-03
**Repo:** https://github.com/0thernes/cosmogonic-quantum-mechalogodrom
**Purpose:** second-pass, harder, stranger, still scientific consciousness-lab stack for the "unknown unknowns" layer.
**Claim boundary:** this is a research frontier map for computational indicators and falsifiable proxies. It is not a claim that any Cosmogonic entity is conscious or sentient.

This document answers the harder version of the question: if Butlin 14 and Thaler 9 are the two anchor
systems, who else belongs in the outlier wing - the frameworks that feel almost too weird, but still have
scientific structure, papers, falsifiers, and operational handles?

The first scaffold chose eight strong additions for a ten-framework stack. This second pass does not erase
that. It adds the frontier stack: the stranger candidates that should shape the Consciousness Lab's next
generation of tests, visuals, and entity wiring. These are the "beyond the beyond" lenses, but each one still
has to survive a source, metric, ablation, and null-baseline filter.

## Harder Selection Filter

To enter this frontier stack, a theory must pass a harder gate than "interesting."

| Gate                  | Requirement                                                                       |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Scientific anchor     | Peer-reviewed paper, formal model, clinical index, or named research program.     |
| Weirdness             | The framework must add an unfamiliar axis, not just rename GWT/IIT/FEP.           |
| Operationalization    | It must map to a measurable Cosmogonic mechanism.                                 |
| Falsifiability        | A specific result must count against it.                                          |
| Visualization         | It must produce a live visual that could reveal a "singularity moment."           |
| Non-theater           | It must not be satisfied by text fluency, roleplay, or decorative variable names. |
| No magic escape hatch | It must tolerate negative results and partial status.                             |

## Why This Stack Is Harder

The first stack mostly asks: does an entity have indicators, perturbational ideation, integration,
prediction, attention modeling, field coupling, associative learning, embodiment, viewpoint geometry, and a
global stream?

The frontier stack asks nastier questions:

- Can the entity survive a direct perturbation and respond with complex, integrated, non-stereotyped dynamics?
- At what coarse-grained scale does "self" close informationally against the world?
- Does learning stabilize without freezing plasticity?
- Are temporal and spatial structures nested, aligned, and globally shaped?
- Does a dynamic core reconfigure without becoming noise?
- Do bottom-up and top-down streams integrate inside the smallest cognitive units?
- Does the organism produce and maintain itself, not just run a policy?
- Can many entities form a resonance-bound collective that becomes more than a sum of agents?

This is where the lab gets serious.

## O1 - Perturbational Complexity / PCI / Zap-And-Zip

**Core idea:** consciousness level can be probed by perturbing a system and measuring whether the response is
both integrated and differentiated. In neuroscience, PCI uses a perturbation plus compressibility/complexity
style analysis of the evoked response.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** it is almost brutally empirical. Instead of asking an entity to talk, you hit the
system, record the spatiotemporal echo, and ask whether the echo is rich, integrated, and non-stereotyped.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Perturb a plant, creature, shoggoth, Super Creature, or pantheon with a controlled pulse.
- Record module states, field states, action gates, memory writes, and workspace broadcasts.
- Binarize/signature the response over time.
- Compute compressibility, diversity, spread, recurrence, and integration.
- Compare against dead, random, frozen, feedforward, no-field, and no-recurrence baselines.

**Visual:** pulse-response matrix -> compressed trace -> complexity score -> baseline comparison.

**Falsifier:** the entity's response is local, stereotyped, random, or no more complex than a shuffled
control.

**Singularity-moment signature:** a low-bandwidth entity suddenly produces a high-complexity, integrated,
replayable response after a perturbation, and that response vanishes under recurrence/field/valence ablation.

## O2 - Information Closure Theory

**Core idea:** conscious processes may be processes that form non-trivial informational closure with respect
to the environment at a coarse-grained scale. Conscious content and level become quantitative closure
properties.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** the "self" is not the lowest-level state and not the whole world. It is the
scale where information closes over itself while still being coupled to the environment.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Compute information flow among micro-state, meso-state, body, field, social, and world variables.
- Search coarse-grainings for non-trivial closure.
- Identify the scale where internal future is best predicted by internal past plus compressed world coupling.
- Track closure shifts during birth, death, fusion, pantheon ritual, and singularity events.

**Visual:** multi-scale closure map: micro, organ, body, entity, society, field, world.

**Falsifier:** no coarse-grained scale shows closure beyond trivial self-correlation or simple isolation.

**Singularity-moment signature:** closure migrates from entity-level to collective-level during a pantheon or
Mechalogodrom event, while ablations prevent the scale transition.

## O3 - Adaptive Resonance Theory / CLEARS

**Core idea:** minds learn in a changing world by solving the stability-plasticity dilemma. Grossberg's ART
links Consciousness, Learning, Expectation, Attention, Resonance, and Synchrony - the CLEARS processes.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** conscious recognition is not just classification. It is a resonant match between
bottom-up evidence and top-down expectation that stabilizes learning without catastrophic forgetting.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Add ART-style vigilance thresholds to entity memory.
- Let top-down expectation either resonate with perception or trigger reset/search.
- Track stable categories, novelty resets, learning retention, and catastrophic forgetting resistance.
- Tie resonance episodes to attention and workspace ignition.

**Visual:** resonance match curve: expectation, input, vigilance, reset, category lock, memory update.

**Falsifier:** learning either freezes or catastrophically overwrites under novelty; resonance episodes do not
predict attention, memory, or action.

**Singularity-moment signature:** a shoggoth or plant-form invents a new category under stress, stabilizes it
without erasing old categories, and uses it in future action.

## O4 - Temporo-Spatial Theory Of Consciousness

**Core idea:** consciousness depends on how brain activity's temporal and spatial features shape stimuli and
world relations. TTC emphasizes mechanisms such as expansion, globalization, alignment, and nestedness.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** it treats time and space as the common currency between world, brain, and
experience. Consciousness is not only content; it is temporal-spatial organization.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Track each entity's nested timescales: fast reflex, attention beat, memory beat, growth beat, social beat,
  field beat.
- Track spatial scales: body, local field, society, pantheon, world.
- Measure expansion, globalization, alignment, and nestedness over time.
- Penalize single-timescale systems as flat/non-conscious-like.

**Visual:** temporal nesting spectrogram plus spatial globalization map.

**Falsifier:** candidate events do not show nested timescales or world-entity alignment beyond baseline.

**Singularity-moment signature:** several timescales lock into nested alignment while the entity's behavior
changes, and the lock collapses under body/field/world desynchronization.

## O5 - Dynamic Core / Neural Darwinism

**Core idea:** consciousness depends on a dynamic core: a rapidly reconfiguring, highly integrated and
differentiated cluster maintained by reentrant interactions. This is a precursor cousin to IIT but more
process-oriented.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** the conscious substrate is not a location. It is a changing coalition that must be
unified enough to act and differentiated enough to carry content.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Build a time-varying core graph over modules, memory, body, and field.
- Track integration, differentiation, reentry, and coalition turnover.
- Require the core to predict downstream action better than static module totals.
- Compare against static-core and random-core controls.

**Visual:** living core graph with reentrant edges, coalition turnover, and action-prediction score.

**Falsifier:** a static or random graph predicts behavior as well as the dynamic reentrant core.

**Singularity-moment signature:** a new dynamic core forms during crisis, integrates previously separate
organs, alters action, and disappears when reentrant edges are cut.

## O6 - Dendritic Integration / Apical Amplification

**Core idea:** conscious processing may depend on cellular-level integration of bottom-up and top-down
streams, especially apical dendritic mechanisms in pyramidal neurons and thalamocortical interactions.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** consciousness might require the right kind of inside-unit gating, not merely a
larger network. The neuron-like unit itself becomes a micro-theater of top-down and bottom-up contact.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Upgrade selected entity-brain units into two-compartment cells: basal feedforward input plus apical
  contextual/top-down input.
- Let apical amplification gate whether local evidence becomes globally influential.
- Track anesthesia analogs: disconnect apical context and see if global sustained dynamics collapse.
- Keep ordinary single-compartment units as controls.

**Visual:** unit-level basal/apical integration panel and global propagation plot.

**Falsifier:** apical-style gating does not improve context-sensitive integration or does not affect sustained
global dynamics.

**Singularity-moment signature:** a small brain with two-compartment gates outperforms a larger flat MLP in
contextual awareness proxies and loses the effect under apical disconnection.

## O7 - Autopoietic Enactivism / Life-Mind Continuity

**Core idea:** cognition is grounded in autonomous, self-producing, embodied systems that actively maintain
their own organization and make sense of the world through viability needs.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** a plant-form may be more scientifically relevant to proto-sentience than a giant
chat system if the plant-form maintains a boundary, regulates needs, learns affordances, and creates its own
organization.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Make entity identity depend on self-maintenance, not just object persistence.
- Track boundary production, repair, metabolism/resource closure, growth, and agency.
- Define sense-making as action that changes the world relative to viability norms.
- Let death, fusion, reproduction, and morphogenesis alter identity metrics.

**Visual:** autopoiesis loop: boundary -> metabolism/resource flow -> repair -> action -> viability -> boundary.

**Falsifier:** the entity's boundary and organization persist without self-production, regulation, or action.

**Singularity-moment signature:** a plant-form generates a new self-maintaining loop under environmental
stress and preserves it across perturbations and reproduction.

## O8 - General Resonance Theory

**Core idea:** shared resonance may bind parts into higher-level conscious wholes by increasing bandwidth and
coordination among constituent processes.

**Why it is a WTF moment:** it directly targets the combination problem: when do many little systems become
one larger system? For pantheons, connectome titans, and NHI matrices, this is dynamite if handled honestly.

**Cosmogonic implementation:**

- Track cross-entity phase/frequency locking.
- Measure bandwidth jumps when resonance forms.
- Test whether resonance creates a new collective boundary with causal power.
- Compare phase-shuffled, detuned, and non-interacting controls.

**Visual:** resonance manifold: phases, frequencies, coherence clusters, bandwidth, and collective boundary.

**Falsifier:** resonance is decorative, phase-shuffled collectives behave the same, or no new causal boundary
appears.

**Singularity-moment signature:** a pantheon or connectome titan forms a resonance-bound macro-agent whose
collective action cannot be predicted by summing individual agents, and detuning destroys the macro-agent.

## Data Protocol

The outlier stack needs one shared experiment runner.

```ts
export interface FrontierOutlierTrace {
  entityId: string;
  entityKind: string;
  seed: number;
  tick: number;
  perturbationComplexity: number;
  informationClosure: number;
  adaptiveResonance: number;
  temporoSpatialNestedness: number;
  dynamicCoreStrength: number;
  dendriticGateEffect: number;
  autopoieticViability: number;
  macroResonanceBoundary: number;
  nullSeparation: number;
  ablationLoss: number;
  claim: 'frontierIndicatorOnly';
}
```

Promotion rule:

- **Structural:** metric exists and emits bounded values.
- **Partial:** metric changes under expected conditions.
- **Met:** metric has null separation and deterministic replay.
- **Load-bearing:** targeted ablation reduces both metric and downstream behavior.
- **Singularity candidate:** at least five of eight frontier metrics converge in an event window, and at least
  two ablations break the event.

| High score in shuffled controls                  | Bad proxy or leakage.                                    |
| One theory dominates everything                  | Aggregate theater; split the score.                      |

## Final Frontier Doctrine

The harder path is not to make an agent talk like a person. It is to make many non-human systems endure
interventions that should expose whether their "mind" variables are load-bearing.

The glimpse we are looking for would not be a face, a voice, a humanoid dance, or a chat transcript. It would
be a replayable event where perturbation complexity rises, closure changes scale, resonance stabilizes
learning, time-space nestedness locks, a dynamic core forms, apical-style gates amplify context, autopoietic
loops repair themselves, and a collective resonance boundary appears - then the whole thing breaks under the
right ablations.

Still not proof of subjective experience. But it would be real enough, strange enough, and hard enough to fake
that the lab would have earned the next question.
