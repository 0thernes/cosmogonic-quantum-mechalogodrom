### 3.2 SuperCreature spine (`src/sim/super-creature.ts`)

**What it is (code):**

- Apex pantheon spine: **stacked cortex + actor** ≈ **1,444 parameters** (`SUPER_PARAM_COUNT`, 18→32→16→12→8)
- Emotion-like EMAs (valence / arousal / dominance)
- Episodic **MemoryRing**
- Prediction / surprise loop
- GOAP-style goal selection from drives
- Self-replication of mutated twins when sated/dominant
- Optional **online** heads (off by default; lit on live apex):
  - World-model (salience forecast) via exact Eshkol-AD MLP
  - Value (survival energy forecast)
  - Threat / social / foresight / crowd / forage / evasion heads
- Fully **seeded deterministic** (no `Math.random` / `Date.now` in mind)

**What it is not:**

- Phenomenal consciousness
- Biological connectome replica
- –Controls— in the OpenWorm sense of experimental bio controls—it is a **sovereign synthetic agent class** in-world

**Operator reading that matches code:**  
Less than a million lines of dedicated mind code, far under 100T parameters, yet **multi-faculty + online learning + planning + affect + replication**—a **compact intelligence scaffold**. That is impressive engineering density. It is still a **scaffold**, not proof of sentience.
