1. Getting started

Open the dome at /. The simulation runs in your browser via Bun + WebGL. Every run is seeded and deterministic: the same seed reproduces the same population trajectory. Reloading the page starts a fresh session for super-creature evolution, levels, and economy — nothing carries over from the last visit.

First five minutes

  • Watch entities move, trade, split, and mutate in the arena.
  • Open Telemetry (left) for population and chaos readouts.
  • Try Sorting Fields — pick a field or hit AUTO to cycle every 6 seconds.
  • Tap dock buttons: AI, HELP, NEURAL, ARCHITECT.
  • Use ⛓ ACCESS when ready to unlock the playable super creature twin.

2. Controls & dock

The bottom persist bar holds DOCS · SPEC · BIBLE · LAB, transport (⏸ ↻ ⏱ 👁 ⬡), sim actions (⇄ ✦ ☢ ⚡), ACCESS, and SET. Camera movement pads live in Settings and on touch overlays (joystick + look pad). Keyboard: WASD move, QE vertical, arrows rotate, Space burst, Shift split, M mutate, Tab chaos boost.

Settings (⚙ SET)

Audio, render mode, view, weather, pause, time scale, reset, apocalypse, singularity, NHI launch, and chaos mode. Split / Burst / Mutate / Chaos moved here from the old control panel to save HUD space.

3. The living world

Up to 50,000 organisms across phyla morphotypes, titans, economy agents, weather, and chaos fields. Entities use real seeded math — colors are quint-prime hashed for thousands of saturated variations. The Mechalogodrom center monster, Archon pantheons, and NHSI faculties read and write shared world state.

4. Super creature & ACCESS

Solving the access puzzle unlocks superhero mode: you become the second super creature. The twin HUD shows life, energy, XP, wallet, neural emotion, powers, and pilot/camera modes. Brains: letter creatures ~25,000 parameters each; APEX #101 starts at 100,000 parameters scaling toward millions — alien, non-earth morphologies documented in the repo MD corpus.

5. 100-letter dome & APEX

The Latin/Greek/Roman alphabet pantheon (100 glyphs + APEX #101) orbits the dome as a visual swarm. Breeding rites no longer wire into petri growth — they exist, glow, and evolve visually until backend coupling returns. The APEX abomination is the capstone visual entity.

6. Sorting fields

Twenty-five algorithms sort entities by different keys (energy, age, hue, etc.). Modes:

  • Single — one field at a time; click rows to switch.
  • ALL — every field active; progress bars pulse together.
  • AUTO — advances to the next field every 6 sim-seconds; readout shows countdown.

7. Neural / Architect / NHSI

The center HUD cycles AI copilot, HELP, audit log, NHSI neural observatory, market, architect panels, and the 101-creature architecture pantheon cycler. Connectome and brain composites are functional correlates — scaffolding for measurement, not claims of sentience. A richer 4D spiking brain visualization is planned inside Architect as a cycle mode.

8. Quantum WildBeyond lab

The Lab artifact runs 36 WEBGL tiles across three boards: collapse fields, attractors (Lorenz, Rössler, Thomas, …), reaction–diffusion, Voronoi, and curl-noise flows. Tap tiles to pulse; page keys switch boards. Mobile tiles use pulled-back cameras so attractors stay framed.
