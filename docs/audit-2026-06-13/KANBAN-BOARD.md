## How to read this board

Cards flow **NOW → NEXT → LATER → BACKLOG** by audit severity and confirmation status:

| Column         | Admission rule                                                                                      |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **🔴 NOW**     | Critical findings **+** confirmed High (the 19-item [`CONFIRMED`] set). Ship-blockers.              |
| **🟠 NEXT**    | Remaining High not in the confirmed-now cut **+** impactful Medium (security/correctness/contract). |
| **🟡 LATER**   | Medium quality, perf headroom, accessibility, docs drift.                                           |
| **⚪ BACKLOG** | Low / Info polish, latent traps, provenance notes.                                                  |

**Card fields:** `Card ID | Title | Severity | Effort | Discipline | Files`

- **Severity:** 🔴 critical · 🟠 high · 🟡 medium · ⚪ low · ▫️ info
- **Effort:** **S** ≤½ day · **M** ½–2 days · **L** > 2 days (or needs an owner decision)
- **Discipline:** **Exec** (THE EXECUTOR — finish/observe), **Architect** (ORACLE — contracts/boundaries),
  **Physicist** (DR MANHATTAN — determinism/measurement), **DevOps**, **Security**

**Card IDs** are stable handles (`SEC-01`, `DET-01`, …) — cite them in commits and PRs.
