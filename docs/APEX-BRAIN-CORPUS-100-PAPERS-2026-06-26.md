## Deductive Rationale

### Axioms

1. **Finite simulation axiom:** everything inside Cosmogonic is a finite-state or finite-precision dynamical
   process, even when it models continuous physics.
2. **Determinism axiom:** for a fixed seed and fixed version, the worldline must reproduce.
3. **No-magic axiom:** real quantum advantage, sentience, phenomenal consciousness, and retrocausality are
   not assumed unless measured externally under accepted scientific standards.
4. **Embodiment axiom:** intelligence-like behavior is stronger when perception, action, memory, and body
   morphology form a closed loop.
5. **Open-endedness axiom:** a biologic creature needs novelty pressure, reproduction/mutation, resource
   limits, and ecological competition, not just a large policy network.
6. **Agency axiom:** the creature should maximize not only reward, but controllable future influence
   (empowerment) and uncertainty reduction (expected free energy).
7. **Thermodynamic axiom:** computation has cost; every thought-like operation must consume a budget, decay a
   resource, or generate a measurable burden.
8. **Alien-umwelt axiom:** non-human cognition should not be judged by human-language competence alone; its
   sensorium and affordance space can be non-human, non-verbal, spatial, topological, acoustic, quantum-like,
   and metabolic.

### Deductions

1. If the creature is deterministic but open-ended, then the weirdness must come from **high-dimensional
   feedback**, not unbounded randomness.
2. If the creature is embodied, then body deformation, sensor placement, resource flow, and action selection
   must be co-computed.
3. If quantum mechanics is simulated classically, then the quantum layer is a **feature generator and geometry
   engine**, not a speedup claim.
4. If consciousness is not demonstrated, then the system may only expose **indicator proxies**: ignition,
   integration/segregation, metacognitive confidence, predictive self-modeling, attention schema, and
   embodied agency.
5. If "alien philosophy" is operational, then it becomes a non-human **umwelt generator**: sensors, affordances,
   valence channels, and representation spaces that do not privilege human text.
6. If the entity must be scalable, then every exotic mechanism needs a cheap proxy, a medium-fidelity sim, and
   an optional high-fidelity backend.

## Inductive Rationale

The literature converges on a practical pattern:

- Artificial life shows that local rules plus selection pressure can produce surprising complexity.
- Reservoir computing shows that rich dynamics can be useful even when the internal substrate is fixed.
- Neuromorphic and physical computing show that body/material dynamics can be computation.
- Active inference and empowerment show how agents can be driven without a single external reward function.
- Consciousness science shows there is no single accepted test, so the correct engineering posture is a
  multi-theory proxy dashboard with strict honesty boundaries.
- Game theory shows that deception, cooperation, domination, and signaling arise naturally in repeated
  multi-agent environments.
- Formal limits show why "solving everything" is impossible; the creature should instead exploit bounded
  approximations and recognize unknown states.

The inductive conclusion: the "abomination" should be a **multi-substrate agency machine**, not a chatbot,
not a simple neural net, and not a literal paranormal quantum system.
