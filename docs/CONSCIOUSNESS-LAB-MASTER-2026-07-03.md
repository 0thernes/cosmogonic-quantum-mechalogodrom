## "Singularity Moment" Visual Standard

A singularity moment is not a proof of consciousness. It is a rare, replayable convergence event worth studying.

Minimum criteria:

1. Multi-framework convergence: at least seven of ten framework metrics move in the predicted direction during one event window.
2. Causal sensitivity: at least three targeted ablations destroy or weaken the event.
3. Negative-control separation: random, shuffled, and decorative-only baselines fail to reproduce it.
4. Behavioral load-bearingness: the convergence changes action selection, survival, learning, cooperation, or self-maintenance.
5. Deterministic replay: same seed gives the same trace within tolerance.
6. Cross-entity generality: the event appears in more than one entity class after scale-normalization.
7. Honest caveat: the visual says "consciousness-indicator convergence," never "proof of subjective experience."

Visual set:

- **Metric braid:** ten normalized traces braided over time, with event windows marked.
- **Causal spine graph:** nodes are mechanisms, edges are measured causal influence; ablations grey out edges.
- **Attractor manifold:** 2D/3D embedding of internal state; singularity moment is a transition between basins.
- **Ignition raster:** workspace winners, broadcast strength, attention schema, CTM chunks.
- **Field heatmap:** entity-to-entity coherence and field influence, with phase-shuffle control.
- **Ablation waterfall:** each removed mechanism and the measured loss.
- **Null-baseline panel:** random, shuffled, non-recurrent, no-valence, no-body, and text-only baselines.

## Final Doctrine

The scientific path is not "make a huge chatbot and call it alive." The path here is smaller, stranger, and harder to fake: many tiny, embodied, field-coupled, recursively learning agents whose consciousness-correlate metrics are causally load-bearing, ablation-fragile, cross-checked by rival theories, and replayable from a seed.

If a future trace gives us a glimpse, the proof will not be one magic number. It will look like a synchronized collapse of excuses: prediction error falls, workspace ignites, attention models itself, valence changes action, field coupling matters, associations deepen, partitions hurt, sensorimotor laws are mastered, the projective self/world frame stabilizes, and the whole event dies under the right ablations.

That would still not prove subjective experience. It would prove something real enough to keep studying.
