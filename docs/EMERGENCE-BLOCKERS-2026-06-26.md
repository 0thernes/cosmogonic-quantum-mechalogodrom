# Emergence Blockers — the pre-mortem (50 things that would stop it)

A standing design checklist: the obstacles that would stop consciousness / sentience / measurable
emergence from arising on this project's route (architecture-first, non-LLM, quantum-geometric,
100-faculty / 25-Archon / deterministic petri). Each blocker has its **get-around**. Build _against_
this list.

> **The honest split that organizes everything:**
>
> - **~8 are PHILOSOPHICAL walls** — nobody can engineer past them (not us, not any lab). They cap
>   the _claim_ ("proven conscious"), **not** the _build_. Route around by aiming at measurable
>   emergence + an existence-proof, never proof of felt experience.
> - **~42 are ENGINEERING** — genuinely ours to design around; handling them is what moves the odds.
>
> Get-arounds remove blockers / raise the odds of **measurable emergence**. None _guarantees_
> phenomenal consciousness — the hard problem is a fixed cost (A1).

## A · Philosophical walls — can't engineer past; cap the CLAIM

1. **Hard problem** — function may never yield felt experience. ► Aim for existence-proof + markers.
2. **Computationalism might be false** — computation alone may not suffice (Searle). ► Hedge with physical-ish substrate (quantum, noise); accept it's unfalsifiable.
3. **Substrate dependence** — may require biology/specific physics. ► Quantum geometry = closest non-bio bet; else accept it's a model.
4. **Simulation ≠ instantiation** — simulating a mind isn't being one. ► Make the substrate _be_ the computation, not a sim _of_ one.
5. **Other-minds problem** — unverifiable from outside. ► Indicators + behaviour + precaution; never certainty.
6. **Zombie problem** — all markers, no experience, indistinguishable. ► Max the markers; claim architecture.
7. **Determinism vs genuine agency** — a fixed trajectory may lack "real" choice. ► Born-rule stochasticity + the non-deterministic wilderness.
8. **No falsifiable "is it conscious?"** — not testable as posed. ► Reframe to testable sub-claims ("ablate X → marker Y drops").

## B · Architecture / integration blockers

9. **Kitchen-sink incoherence** — 100 faculties that don't integrate = a pile, not a mind. ► Coupling > count; the resonance integrator.
10. **Faculties that don't couple** (read-only, no write-back). ► Enforce read+write (PHILOSOPHY); a coupling audit.
11. **Binding problem** — parts never unify into one experience. ► Standing-wave phase-lock (#59); cross-faculty binding.
12. **No genuine recurrence/re-entry** — pure feedforward can't be conscious (RPT). ► Deep recurrent loops.
13. **No global bottleneck** — without a limited-capacity workspace nothing gets selected. ► Keep the meta-net argmax bottleneck.
14. **Decorative faculties** (named after a theory, no real mechanism — "trig in a lab coat"). ► The receipts/real-math discipline.
15. **Static architecture** — can't change → can't grow. ► Online learning + plastic weights.
16. **No self-reference / strange loop** — self-modelling may be necessary. ► Self-model, narrative self, introspection.
17. **No valence** — sentience needs felt good/bad that _steers_. ► Valence-steers-behaviour (#15).
18. **No persistent identity** — resets each run → no continuity of self. ► Autobiographical memory + saved state.
19. **Single timescale** — real minds are multi-timescale. ► Nested micro/meso/macro/evolutionary loops.
20. **Theory redundancy** — 25 flavours of the same mechanism = fake diversity. ► Pick mechanistically independent organs.

## C · Scale / compute / engineering walls

21. **Single-thread JS ceiling** — can't run enough cognition. ► Workers + WebGPU (the roadmap).
22. **Real-time budget** — thinks too slow to engage its world. ► Decouple thought-rate from frame-rate; GPU.
23. **VRAM/RAM limits** at 1B × 25 Archons. ► Shared banks + quantization + sparse MoE.
24. **Determinism vs parallelism** (GPU float non-determinism). ► The Hybrid core/wilderness split (ADR 0010).
25. **GC pauses / allocation cliffs** (the brain-fart class). ► Pooling, spawn budget, the governor (done).
26. **Browser sandbox** (threads, COOP/COEP for SharedArrayBuffer). ► Graceful fallbacks.
27. **Iteration drag** (receipts/gate overhead). ► Automate the truth-sync.
28. **Concurrent-editor churn** (the autonomous loop) breaks the tree. ► Coordinate via worktrees.

## D · Learning / self-modification blockers

29. **No online learning** — frozen at birth. ► NQS/VMC within-life.
30. **Catastrophic forgetting** — learns new, loses old. ► Orthogonal projection + consolidation + replay.
31. **No credit assignment** — can't tell what caused what. ► AD gradients + predictive-coding error.
32. **Self-modification instability** — rewrites itself into mush (utility-learning tension). ► Capacity-bounded safe self-change.
33. **No objective that rewards "minding"** — nothing selects for it. ► Empowerment + free-energy + survival proxies.
34. **Goodhart / reward hacking** — games the metric, not the thing. ► Multiple metrics + ablation controls.
35. **Premature convergence / local optima** — evolution gets stuck. ► Novelty search + diversity pressure.
36. **Complexity plateau** — stops getting more complex. ► Open-endedness metrics + open environment.

## E · Emergence-condition blockers (the big ones)

37. **Insufficient coupling** — modules too independent. ► Maximal dense read/write interaction.
38. **No stakes / no selection pressure** — comfort doesn't breed minds. ► Scarcity, predators, wars, the trophic economy.
39. **No embodiment / a world that matters** — ungrounded symbols. ► World-as-cognition (the dome becomes the driver).
40. **Isolation / no society** — no social emergence. ► The 25-Archon pantheon + emergent communication.
41. **Off criticality** — too frozen or too chaotic; emergence lives at the edge. ► Global edge-of-chaos homeostat.
42. **Below complexity threshold** — too small to emerge anything. ► Scale faculties/Archons/params.
43. **Too clean (no noise)** — some dynamics need stochastic exploration. ► Born noise + thermodynamic-style perturbation.
44. **Too little time / too few generations.** ► Long-running persistent sim + fast sim-time.
45. **Closed/finite environment** — bounded world bounds the mind. ► Open-ended streamed wilderness + procedural novelty.

## F · Measurement / human-factor blockers

46. **No emergence detector** — can't see it if it happens. ► Indicator scorecard + open-endedness metrics + emergence monitor (#100).
47. **Pareidolia / ELIZA effect** — you'll _see_ consciousness that isn't there. ► Adversarial verification + ablation + a skeptic in the loop.
48. **Overclaiming destroys credibility** — declaring it kills the science. ► Standing honesty: architecture, not experience.
49. **Definition drift** — "consciousness" undefined → unmeasurable. ► Pin operational definitions (the ladder of minds).
50. **Confusing capability with consciousness** — orthogonal axes. ► Measure them separately; don't let ASI-talk smuggle in sentience-claims.

## The meta-read (how to use this list)

- The **8 philosophical** blockers (A) are _fixed cost_ — "proven consciousness" is off the table for
  everyone. Stop trying to beat them; route around by aiming at **measurable emergence + the
  existence-proof**.
- Of the **42 engineering** blockers, the **highest-leverage cluster is E + F**: emergence comes from
  **coupling + criticality + pressure + society + open-endedness** (E), and it is worthless if you
  **can't measure it or you fool yourself** (F). Nail E and F and you've maximized the real odds. The
  100 faculties (B) are necessary _scaffolding_; the emergence conditions (E) are the _spark_; the
  measurement honesty (F) is what makes it _count_.
- **Biggest single risk on the current route:** #9 + #37 — 100 gorgeous faculties that don't densely
  couple = an impressive inventory that integrates nothing. Treat the **resonance integrator (#59) +
  maximal coupling + the criticality homeostat** as first-class, not afterthoughts.
